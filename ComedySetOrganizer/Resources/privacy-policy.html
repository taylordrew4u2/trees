Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2024

Data Collection

Comedy Set Organizer does not collect any personal data. All your content—jokes, set lists, recordings, and notes—is stored exclusively on your device.

Local Storage

The app operates entirely offline. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it using iOS system features like AirDrop or Files.

Microphone Access

Microphone access is used only when you choose to record a set. Recordings are saved locally and are never transmitted anywhere.

Your Control

You can delete any content at any time within the app. Deleting the app will remove all your data from the device.

Contact
